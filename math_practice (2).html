Pythagoras Theorem
a² + b² = c² — find the missing side of a right triangle
🪜 A ladder leans against a wall. The wall is 9m tall and the base is 12m away. How long is the ladder?
1
9² + 12² = c²
2
81 + 144 = 225
3
c = √225
✅ c = 15m
⛺ A tent pole is 8m tall. A rope goes from the top to a peg 6m away. How long is the rope?
1
6² + 8² = c²
2
36 + 64 = 100
3
c = √100
✅ c = 10m
🏈 A field is 30m long and 40m wide. What is the diagonal distance corner to corner?
1
30² + 40² = c²
2
900 + 1600 = 2500
3
c = √2500
✅ c = 50m
🖥️ A TV screen is 24cm tall and 32cm wide. What is the diagonal size of the screen?
1
24² + 32² = c²
2
576 + 1024 = 1600
3
c = √1600
✅ c = 40cm
💡 Remember
c is always the hypotenuse — the longest side, opposite the right angle. Square both known sides, add them, then square root!

Negative Numbers
Adding and subtracting with negative numbers
🌡️ The temperature is −3°C. It drops by 5 more degrees. What is the new temperature?
1
−3 − 5 = −8
✅ −8°C
💳 You owe −€12 in your account. You earn €20. What's your new balance?
1
−12 + 20 = +8
✅ +€8
🏔️ A diver is at −200m below sea level. He swims up 350m. What is his new depth?
1
−200 + 350 = +150
✅ +150m above sea level
🎮 Your game score is −15 points. You lose 8 more points. What is your score?
1
−15 − 8 = −23
✅ −23 points
📏 Number Line — moving left = negative, moving right = positive
−10 −5 0 5 +10
⬅ negative positive ➡

Two-Step Equations
Solve for x by doing the same thing to both sides
Solve for x:
2x + 3 = 11
1
Subtract 3 → 2x = 8
2
Divide by 2 → x = 4
✅ x = 4
Solve for x:
3x − 5 = 16
1
Add 5 → 3x = 21
2
Divide by 3 → x = 7
✅ x = 7
Solve for x:
4x + 6 = 22
1
Subtract 6 → 4x = 16
2
Divide by 4 → x = 4
✅ x = 4
Solve for x:
5x − 10 = 30
1
Add 10 → 5x = 40
2
Divide by 5 → x = 8
✅ x = 8
💡 The Golden Rule
Whatever you do to one side of the equation, you must do to the other side too. Think of it like a balance scale — both sides must always stay equal.

Percentages
Discounts, tax, tips and real-world money problems
🛍️ Shoes cost €80. There is a 25% discount. What is the final price?
You pay 75% of €80
€60 (75%)
1
80 × 0.25 = €20 off
2
80 − 20 = €60
✅ €60
🍕 A pizza costs €12. There is 10% tax. What is the total?
You pay 110% of €12
€13.20 (110%)
1
12 × 0.10 = €1.20 tax
2
12 + 1.20 = €13.20
✅ €13.20
🧥 A jacket costs €120. It's on sale for 30% off. What do you pay?
You pay 70% of €120
€84 (70%)
1
120 × 0.30 = €36 off
2
120 − 36 = €84
✅ €84
🍔 A meal costs €25. You leave a 20% tip. How much tip do you leave?
Tip = 20% of €25
€5
1
25 × 0.20 = €5
✅ Tip = €5
💡 Quick Tip
Convert any percentage to a decimal first: 25% → 0.25 · 10% → 0.10 · 30% → 0.30. Then multiply!
